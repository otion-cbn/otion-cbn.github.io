Troubleshooting

Username and Password Troubleshooting

What can I do if I forgot my password?

To reset your password, first open the Otion app. On the sign in screen, tap Forgot Password below Log In. From there then follow the on-screen instructions.

I can't locate my account

If you aren't able to locate your account after entering your email:
  • Make sure that you're entering your email correctly, especially if it contains repeated characters
If you think your email was changed as a result of your account being compromised:
  • Check if you've received an email from Otion notifying you that your account information was changed

What can I do if I receive a password reset email but still can't sign in.

If you've received an email but still aren't able to log in, try sending yourself a password reset email again.

If you're copying the reset link or your new password, make sure you aren't including any extra spaces before pasting it into your browser or the login fields.

I think my Otion account has been hacked.

Send us a message here immediately.

Email Troubleshooting

Can I change my email?

No, your email cannot be changed. If you wish to do so, you will need to contact us here.

What can I do if I don't receive a password reset email?

If you're not receiving the password reset email, it's possible that the email is going in your email's spam filter or that you mistyped your email when you first signed up for Otion.

To find your password reset email, try:
  1. Checking the spam filter on your email account
  2. Getting in touch with your email service provider to make sure that you're able to get emails from the @firebaseapp.com domain
If you mistyped your email address when signing up for your account, we're not able to give you access to this account.

What can I do if I've lost access to the email I signed up with?

If you lost access to the email address you used to sign up for Otion, you can send us a message here. If you've linked your account to Facebook, you can reset your password using Facebook.

If you can't log into Otion because you forgot your password and can't access the email address you used to sign up, please get in touch with your email service provider to get access to that email.

If you've forgotten your password and lost access to your email account, we're unable to release the Instagram account to you. This includes accounts registered with mistyped email addresses.

We understand this can be frustrating, but this policy is meant to make sure Otion accounts don't fall into the wrong hands.
