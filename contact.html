How do I add a contact on Otion?

To add a contact:
  • Go to your contact by tapping .
  • Click on the upper side, then choose Add New Contact.
  • Scan your friend's QR Code and click Save.
If you're having issues with adding new contact, let us know.

Is my contact public?

No, your contact is personal and different from the others.
