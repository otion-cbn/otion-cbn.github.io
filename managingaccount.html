Managing Your Account

Otion Settings

How do I log out of Otion?

You can log out of the Otion app by going to More.
  1. Go to your profile
  2. Tap (iPhone) or (Android) in the top-right corner
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the page and tap Log Out
If you're having trouble logging in, try these tips.

When does Otion send push notifications to my device?

We may send notifications for several reasons, including:
  • Someone liking or commenting on one of your photos or videos
  • Someone mentioning you in a comment
You can adjust your push notifications from your account settings.

Phone Settings

Where can I adjust Otion settings on my Android?

To adjust settings for Otion on Android, including uninstalling the app and clearing your cache and data:
  • Leave the Otion app
  • Open the Settings on your phone
  • Tap Applications > Manage Applications > Otion
You'll see options to uninstall (remove Instagram from your phone), clear your cache and clear your data (reset all of the memory on your phone).
