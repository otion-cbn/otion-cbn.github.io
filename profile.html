Edit Your Profile

How do I add a bio and other information?

You will be asked to fill your profile info when you create your account on the first time.

How do I update profile information like my name, photo and bio?

To update your profile information:
  • Go to your profile by tapping .
  • Tap Edit Profile.
  • Type in your new name, website, bio or other information, then tap Done or Save in the top right.

How do I change my profile picture?

To add or change your profile picture:
  1. Go to your profile by tapping .
  2. Tap your profile picture. If you don't have a profile picture, tap the blank circle next to your name.
  3. Choose your desired picture.
