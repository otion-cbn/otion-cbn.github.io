Getting Started

Creating an Account

How do I create an Otion account?

Otion is a mobile app that you can download on iOS and Android phones and tablets. To create an Otion account:
  1. Download the Otion app for iOS from the App Store or Android from Google Play Store.
  2. Once the app is installed, tap to open it.
  3. Go to Sign Up page, then enter your email address and tap Next. You can also tap Sign in with Facebook to sign up with your Facebook account or Sign in with Google to sign up with your Google account.
  4. If you register with email, enter verification code, fill out your profile info and then tap Done. If you register with Facebook, you'll be prompted to log into your Facebook account if you're currently logged out.
If you sign up with email, make sure you enter your email address correctly and choose an email address that only you can access. If you log out and forget your password, you'll need to be able to access your email to get back into your Otion account.

I received an email that I created a new Otion account, but I didn't sign up for a new account.

Most likely, someone tried to sign up for an Otion account using your email address by mistake. To remove your email from the account:
  1. If you have an Otion account, log out. Before logging out, make sure the you have access to the email address currently associated with your account. If you don't have an Otion account, skip to step 2.
  2. Send us a message here to let us know this wasn't you.
You should then be able to use this email address to sign up for an Otion account.

If you're still having issues with your account, let us know.

Navigating the App

Sign Up Screen

This sign up screen lets you create a new account using email only. If you want to create account using Facebook or Google, please choose the sign in option in Sign In page. After you create an account, please go to your email and verify if it's you.

Sign In Screen

The sign in screen lets you create a new account or log into an existing account. You can also recover your password by tapping Forgot Password below the sign in button.

Keep in mind that when logging into an existing account you must use the email and password for the account. Learn more about sign in issues here.

Profile

Profile shows your bio and other information. It's also where you can edit your profile info and link your account to social media.
